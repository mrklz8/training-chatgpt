V4 Boulderer Training Routine

Goals:

  • Improve technique, strength, and power to advance from V4 to higher grades.
  • Increase finger strength and endurance.
  • Develop efficient movement on the wall.

Training Focus:

1. Warm-Up (10-15 minutes)

  • Dynamic stretching: Arm circles, leg swings.
  • Easy climbing on V0-V2 problems to warm up fingers and body.

2. Strength Training (2 times per week)

  • Hangboard Training:
    • Exercise: 5-7 second hangs with 3-minute rest, 3 sets.
    • Grips: Open hand, half crimp.
    • Progression: Increase hang time or reduce rest as strength improves.
  • Core Strength:
    • Planks, leg raises, and other core exercises.
    • 3 sets of 10-12 reps each.

3. Technique (3-4 times per week)

  • Climbing Sessions:
    • Focus on technique and movement efficiency.
    • Practice footwork drills and body positioning.
  • Project V4 Boulders:
    • Work on mastering sequences and refining beta.
    • Analyze and adjust tactics with each attempt.

4. Power and Dynamic Movement (1-2 times per week)

  • Limit Bouldering:
    • Work on challenging boulders close to your limit (V3-V4).
    • Focus on powerful moves and dynamic transitions.
  • Dyno Training:
    • Practice basic dynos and dynamic footwork.
    • Start with controlled movements and progress to more dynamic moves.
  • 4x4 Power Endurance:
    • Perform four sets of four-minute climbing intervals.
    • Choose problems at your limit (V2-V3) and climb continuously.
    • Rest 3-4 minutes between sets.

5. Endurance and Recovery (1-2 times per week)

  • Endurance Climbing:
    • ARC (Aerobic Restoration and Capillarity) training for 10-15 minutes.
    • Climb continuously on easier problems to build endurance.
  • Active Recovery:
    • Restorative yoga or light stretching sessions.
    • Focus on flexibility and muscle relaxation.

Weekly Schedule Example:

  • Monday: Hangboard training + Core strength.
  • Tuesday: Technique session (Climbing drills).
  • Wednesday: Rest day or active recovery.
  • Thursday: Limit bouldering session + 4x4 Power Endurance.
  • Friday: Technique session (Project V4 boulders).
  • Saturday: Power session (Dyno training).
  • Sunday: Endurance climbing (ARC training) + Restorative yoga.
